## What Is IPTV?

IPTV stands for **Internet Protocol Television**. Unlike traditional cable or satellite TV that broadcasts signals in real-time, IPTV delivers television content over your internet connection. Think of it as streaming TV channels — but through your broadband line, not a cable box.

### How It Works

1. TV channels are encoded into IP packets at the headend.
2. These packets travel over our fibre-optic network to your router.
3. A **set-top box (STB)** connected to your router decodes the packets and displays channels on your TV.

Because it uses your existing broadband connection, there's no need for separate cable wiring or a dish antenna.

## IPTV vs. Traditional Cable TV

| Feature | Cable TV | IPTV |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Channel Selection | Fixed bundles | Flexible packages |
| Interactive Features | Limited | Electronic Program Guide, catch-up, on-demand |
| Infrastructure | Coaxial cables or dish | Uses your existing fibre connection |
| Reliability | Weather-dependent (satellite) | Weather-independent |
| Cost | Often hidden charges | Transparent pricing |
