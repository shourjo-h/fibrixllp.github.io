We're thrilled to officially welcome you to **Fibrix Broadband** — your trusted high-speed fiber internet provider, right here in South 24 Parganas, West Bengal.

## Who We Are

Fibrix Broadband is the consumer-facing brand of **Fibrix LLP**, a registered Limited Liability Partnership. We deliver blazing-fast FTTH (Fiber-to-the-Home) broadband, enterprise leased lines, IPTV packages, OTT subscriptions, and professional networking hardware — all under one roof.

## Our Mission: Internet for All

We believe everyone deserves access to fast, reliable internet — regardless of where they live. Whether you're in the heart of a town or a quiet village, Fibrix Broadband is committed to bridging the digital divide.

Our tagline says it all: _"The best available out there."_

## What We Offer

- **Home Broadband (FTTH):** Plans starting from just ₹330/month with speeds up to 100 Mbps and unlimited data.
- **Business Leased Lines:** Dedicated, symmetric bandwidth with SLA-backed uptime guarantees.
- **OTT Bundles:** Up to 29 premium streaming platforms included with select plans — JioHotstar, SonyLIV, Zee5, Discovery+, and more.
- **IPTV Packages:** HD channel packages starting at ₹90/month with up to 95 channels.
- **Networking Hardware:** Professional-grade routers, switches, OLTs, ONUs, fiber cables, and more at competitive prices.

## Why Choose Us?

- **99.9% Uptime** — Our GPON-based fiber network is built for reliability.
- **24/7 Support** — We're always here when you need us.
- **Local Presence** — We're based in South 24 Parganas, so you get fast on-ground support.
- **Transparent Pricing** — No hidden charges, no throttling, no surprises.
