About Fibrix Broadband

Your trusted internet partner - connecting homes, businesses, and communities

Who We Are

Fibrix Broadband is the brand name under which Fibrix LLP operates its broadband and internet services. All official documentation, registrations, and legal correspondence are under the name Fibrix LLP - a registered Limited Liability Partnership based in Joynagar Mojilpur, South 24 Parganas, West Bengal.

We provide high-speed fiber broadband internet connectivity to homes, businesses, and communities across the region, along with enterprise-grade networking solutions. We are committed to bridging the digital divide by delivering affordable, reliable internet.

Beyond connectivity, we are also a trusted supplier of professional networking hardware - from GPON/EPON OLT equipment and ONU/ONT devices to fiber optic cables, routers, switches, patch cords, connectors, splitters, and set-top boxes. We serve individual consumers, local cable operators, enterprise clients, and fellow service providers with equal dedication.

What We Do

  • Home Broadband (FTTH): High-speed fiber-to-the-home connections with unlimited data plans, ranging from 30 Mbps to 1 Gbps.
  • Business / Enterprise Leased Lines: Dedicated, symmetric bandwidth with SLA-backed uptime guarantees, static IPs, and managed connectivity for offices, enterprises, and institutions.
  • Wholesale Bandwidth: Bulk internet bandwidth for local cable operators and service providers looking for a reliable upstream partner.
  • OTT Subscriptions: Bundled streaming platform subscriptions with select broadband plans for an all-in-one entertainment and connectivity experience.
  • Networking Hardware Sales: A comprehensive catalogue of networking equipment including routers, switches, OLT systems, ONU/ONT devices, fiber optic cables, patch cords, connectors, splitters, and set-top boxes.
  • Installation & Technical Support: Professional on-site installation, network commissioning, and round-the-clock technical support.

Our Infrastructure

Fibrix Broadband operates a modern GPON-based fiber optic network designed for maximum throughput and minimal latency. Our infrastructure includes:

  • High-capacity OLT (Optical Line Terminal) equipment at our central office
  • Extensive fiber optic backbone spanning our coverage areas
  • Redundant upstream connectivity for failover protection
  • 24/7 Network Operations Centre (NOC) monitoring
  • Industry-standard ONU/ONT customer premises equipment
